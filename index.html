Unlocking the Topographic Secrets of West Bengal

Welcome to the West Bengal Morphometric Analysis Portal, a cutting-edge platform designed to explore the intricate terrain characteristics of West Bengal's 23 districts through advanced geospatial computation. This tool quantifies critical morphometric parameters including Absolute Relief, Relative Relief, Topographic Ruggedness Index (TRI), Dissection Index, Drainage Density and Stream Frequencyproviding unprecedented insights into the state's geomorphological dynamics.


Our platform serves researchers, policymakers, and environmental planners with:

  • District-wise comparative analysis of terrain complexity
  • Visualization of erosional patterns through Dissection Index
  • Hydrological characterization via Drainage Density metrics
  • Quantitative assessment of slope instability risks using TRI

Using our interactive WebGIS interface, simply select your district of interest and morphometric parameter to generate dynamic spatial visualizations. The system automatically redirects to detailed analytical maps with elevation models, drainage networks, and parameter-specific classifications, enabling both scientific research and practical applications in flood management, soil conservation, and infrastructure planning.
